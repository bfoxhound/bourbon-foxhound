# Bourbon 101 - Educational Content

## Getting Started

### What is Bourbon?
Bourbon is America's native spirit. By law, it must be:
- Made in the USA (not just Kentucky!)
- At least 51% corn in the mashbill
- Aged in new, charred oak barrels
- Distilled to no more than 160 proof
- Entered into barrels at no more than 125 proof
- Bottled at minimum 80 proof

### Key Terms
- **Mashbill**: The grain recipe (corn, rye, wheat, barley)
- **Proof**: Alcohol content (2x the percentage)
- **Straight**: Aged at least 2 years
- **Bottled-in-Bond**: 100 proof, aged 4+ years, one distillery/season
- **Single Barrel**: From one specific barrel
- **Small Batch**: Blend of select barrels (vague term)

## Tasting Guide

### The 5 S's of Bourbon Tasting
1. **See**: Color indicates age and barrel char
2. **Swirl**: Watch the "legs" - slower = higher proof/more body
3. **Sniff**: Nose for vanilla, caramel, oak, fruit, spice
4. **Sip**: Small amount, let coat your tongue
5. **Savor**: Note the finish (how long flavors linger)

### Common Flavor Profiles
- **High corn**: Sweet, vanilla, caramel
- **High rye**: Spicy, peppery, bold
- **Wheated**: Soft, smooth, easy-drinking
- **High proof**: Intense, warming, complex

## Distillery Profiles

### Buffalo Trace (Frankfort, KY)
- **Famous for**: Pappy Van Winkle, George T. Stagg, Eagle Rare
- **Style**: Traditional, wheated and rye recipes
- **Tour worth it?**: Yes - historic distillery, free tour

### Heaven Hill (Bardstown, KY)
- **Famous for**: Elijah Craig, Evan Williams, Henry McKenna
- **Style**: High rye, value and premium tiers
- **Tour worth it?**: Yes - great visitor center

### Maker's Mark (Loretto, KY)
- **Famous for**: Maker's Mark, Maker's 46, Cask Strength
- **Style**: Wheated, approachable
- **Tour worth it?**: Yes - hand-dip your own bottle

### Wild Turkey (Lawrenceburg, KY)
- **Famous for**: 101, Rare Breed, Master's Keep
- **Style**: High rye, bold, spicy
- **Tour worth it?**: Yes - great tasting experiences

### Woodford Reserve (Versailles, KY)
- **Famous for**: Woodford Reserve, Double Oaked
- **Style**: Balanced, triple-distilled
- **Tour worth it?**: Yes - beautiful grounds

## How to Read a Label

### Age Statements
- "10 Year" = minimum age of youngest whiskey
- No age statement = at least 4 years (if straight)
- "NAS" (No Age Statement) becoming common

### Proof Guide
- 80-90: Standard, smooth
- 90-100: Full flavor
- 100-115: High proof, intense
- 115+: Cask strength, add water

## Collecting vs. Drinking

### The Hunt
- Allocated bottles (Pappy, BTAC) require luck/relationships
- Store picks (single barrel selections) offer unique experiences
- Limited releases drop quarterly

### Secondary Market
- Legal gray area (selling alcohol without license)
- Facebook groups, auction sites exist
- Prices often 2-10x MSRP for allocated bottles

### Our Advice
- Drink what you like, not what's hyped
- Start with $30-60 bottles to learn your palate
- Don't chase allocated bottles until you know what you enjoy
- Keep a journal (or use our app!) to track tastings
